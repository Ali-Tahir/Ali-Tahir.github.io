Useful Student Benefits

2022-02-20
Learning / Development

There are a lot of benefits for students out there that most people don’t know about. I’m sharing what I found and used.

01 - GitHub Student Developer Pack

You will get all the tools you’ll need to build and ship your projects like a star while you are a student. All you need is a University email that you’ll use to create a GitHub account or make it as primary email in your existing GitHub account and you are good to go for the application!

You will be asked to provide proof that you are currently, a student, and as they say, the whole process of approval can take up to 24 hours once you submit your application. I recommend checking your email once you applied you might get instant access like myself.

Link: https://education.github.com/pack

Some of the many cool things you’ll get for free.

  • Free domain name registration for upto 1-year for your web projects .
  • Free DigitalOcean hosting.
  • Free subscription for few months to famous learning platforms like Pluralsight, DataCamp, Educative etc. that will help you learn and build all kind of great stuff.
  • Free access to Codecov on public and private repositories.
  • Travis CI private builds for free while you’re a student.
